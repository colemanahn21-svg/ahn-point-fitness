Recovery Rules
● GREEN (67%+)
Lift: Full session, push RPE to top of target range.
Finisher: Full ab circuit (2 rounds) or full cardio.
Nutrition: Training day cals (~2,300). Full carbs.
● YELLOW (34–66%)
Lift: Full session. Mid-range RPE (7–8). No PRs.
Finisher: Ab circuit 2 rounds. Cardio 12–15 min only.
Nutrition: Training day cals. Prioritise protein timing.
● RED (<34%)
Lift: Drop 1 set per exercise. RPE 6–7 max.
Finisher: Skip ab circuit AND cardio entirely.
Nutrition: Eat at maintenance. Extra carbs + sleep tonight.

3-Day Red Rule

If WHOOP shows red 3+ days in a row, take a full rest day. Your HRV is likely below 35 ms. Resume when you see yellow or green.

HRV & VO2max Strategy

How This Plan Raises Your HRV

Sleep: Moving bedtime from 1:30→12:30 AM should lift HRV 8–15 ms within 2–3 weeks (your data: 7.5+ hrs = 67% recovery vs 31% under 5.5 hrs).

Zone 2 cardio 3x/wk: Builds parasympathetic tone. Expect VO2max to improve 3–5% over 8 weeks.

Never to failure: RPE 7–8.5 preserves hypertrophy stimulus while allowing faster HRV recovery.

Target: Move 30-day HRV from 44 ms → 55–60 ms by Week 8.

VO2max Push (Weeks 5–8)

During Friday's cardio finisher, add 3–4 × 30-second pushes to Zone 3–4 (150–170 bpm) with 90s easy recovery between. This is your weekly cardiac overload stimulus.
